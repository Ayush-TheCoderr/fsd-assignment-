Key Features

Advanced Health Monitoring

Track heart rate, blood oxygen levels, sleep quality, and stress levels with medical-grade accuracy.

Fitness Tracking

Supports over 100 workout modes with automatic detection for common activities.

Extended Battery Life

Up to 5 days of battery life on a single charge with regular use.

Water Resistant

5 ATM water resistance rating, suitable for swimming and water activities.

Built-in GPS

Track your routes and activities without needing to bring your phone.

Smart Notifications

Receive and respond to messages, calls, and app notifications directly from your wrist.
