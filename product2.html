The TechWrist Sport is designed for athletes and fitness enthusiasts who demand more from their wearable technology. Built with a rugged and lightweight design, this smartwatch is the perfect companion for any workout or outdoor adventure.

Color:

Black

Key Features

Advanced Fitness Tracking

Tracks over 120 different sports and activities with detailed metrics for each workout type.

Altitude & Barometric Tracking

Track elevation changes and atmospheric pressure for hiking and climbing activities.

10 ATM Water Resistance

Suitable for swimming, diving, and water sports up to 100 meters depth.

Extended Battery Life

Up to 7 days of battery life in standard mode, 20 hours in continuous GPS tracking.

Recovery Advisor

Advanced algorithms analyze your workout data to suggest optimal recovery times.

Offline Maps

Download trail maps for hiking and running routes when you're off the grid.
